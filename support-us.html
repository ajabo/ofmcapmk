WAYS TO SUPPORT US

Your support helps the Capuchins' mission to feed the hungry, help the poor, and serve the needs of parishioners in the Province.

The goal of the Capuchin Mission Development Office is to fund the domestic mission of the Province of Mary "Kidane Meherate". That mission ranges from support of our own members in the Province to assistance our local Church institutions.

There are many ways you can support the Province — monthly giving, honor and memorial giving, planned giving, and our educational fund. If you are interested in helping with any of these specific needs, please contact us at (+251) 115-536890 or cappastoralproject@gmail.com
